Welcome to the EcoFood Saver

Our platform is dedicated to helping reduce food waste and ensuring that excess food is properly distributed to those in need. We work with individuals, restaurants, grocery stores, and food banks to create a more sustainable world.

By using our platform, you can track food waste, donate surplus food, and contribute to a better future. Whether you're a business or an individual, every contribution makes a difference.
